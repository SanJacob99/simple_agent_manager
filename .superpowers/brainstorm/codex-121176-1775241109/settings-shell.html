Which settings shell feels right for this app?

You asked for app-level settings now, with room for broader defaults and preferences later. This question is only about where that experience should live in the product.

Recommended: A because it scales best as settings grow.

A. Dedicated settings workspace

Clicking the gear takes you to a full app-level settings page with its own left section nav for API keys, model sync, storage, and future defaults.

B. Large slide-over sheet

Keep the canvas visible underneath and open settings as a broad right-side sheet. It feels close to the current modal, but can get crowded as sections expand.

C. Split settings plus live side panel

Use a full page, but reserve space for contextual help, previews, or health indicators. More powerful, but also busier and heavier for a first version.
